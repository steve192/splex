What happens if my account is deleted?
- Your profile and login credentials are permanently removed.
- Groups where you are the only registered member are deleted.
- In shared groups your past expenses remain visible to other members,
  but are no longer linked to a Splex account.
- You can create a new account at any time — it will not be linked to
  the data from your previous account.
